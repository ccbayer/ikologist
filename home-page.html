Introduction

An award-winning creative leader with over 20 years of experience, specializing in the healthcare space

Hi, my name is Ikuko Aoki-Salinas, known as Iko (e-ko). I’m a creative professional with over 20 years of experience. Originally from Japan, my creative ideas and designs are influenced by diverse cultures, people, and art from around the world.

Throughout my career, I’ve gained broad industry experience across education, financial services, hospitality, and more. Over the past decade, I’ve focused on integrated marketing and branding in the healthcare space.

I love what I do and strive to be more creative every day!
