Introduction

An award-winning creative leader with over 20 years of experience in healthcare

Hi, I’m Ikuko Aoki-Salinas, but most people call me Iko (e-ko). I’m a creative professional specializing in design and creative direction. Originally from Japan, I draw inspiration from diverse cultures, people, and art, which shapes how I think and create.

I’ve worked across healthcare, education, financial services, and hospitality. Over the past decade, my focus has been on integrated marketing and branding in healthcare. I enjoy collaborating with teams, guiding creative direction, and bringing complex ideas to life.

I truly love what I do and strive to stay curious and creative every day.
