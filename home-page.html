Introduction

An award-winning creative professional with over 19 years of experience

Hi, my name is Ikuko Aoki-Salinas, known as Iko (e-ko). I am a creative professional with over 19 years of experience. I am originally from Japan, and my creative ideas and designs are all influenced by different cultures, people, and arts from around the world.

My focus is in intergrated marketing and branding. I have worked in a wide range of industries, such as education, financial services, hospitality, healthcare, and pharmaceuticals. My clients have ranged from small to Fortune 500 companies.

I love what I do and strive to be more creative everyday!
